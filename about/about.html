What's Algerian Techno Movement

The Algerian Techno Movement (ATM) is a collective of DJs, producers, and other artists passionate about underground electronic music in Algeria. Their goal is to promote access to techno and other electronic genres in Algeria while highlighting local artists and sounds. To achieve this objective, ATM strives to create a dynamic and inclusive electronic scene, despite the challenges posed by the country's environment and music industry. What sets the Algerian Techno Movement apart is their commitment to musical diversity and artistic expression. Members of ATM seek to explore new creative avenues in electronic music, drawing from their country's national identity through a musical, folkloric, cinematic, or architectural repertoire. Their visual and sonic research and experimentation allow them to create a unique identity that reflects their individuality. The Algerian Techno Movement is a force for change in the Algerian music scene, seeking to transcend cultural barriers and norms imposed by the music industry. They wish to showcase the cultural riches of their country while creating an open and welcoming community for electronic music enthusiasts in Algeria.

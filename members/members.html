A.K.M / 3abdelkader
  • Artist Name: A.K.M / 3abdelkader
  • Profession: DJ/Producer/Live Performer
  • Region: Algiers, Algeria
  • A.K.M (Abdelkader MOUSSOUS) is a talented Algerian DJ, producer, and performer. Drawing inspiration from artists like Klaus Schulz, Aphex Twin, and Jeff Mills, he creates unique underground electronic music. At just nine years old, he discovered his first music production software. At sixteen, he met and learned from Dj Bokko, known as the pioneer of electronic music in Algeria.
  • A.K.M then dedicated himself to production, developing a tribal and Mediterranean techno style under the name AKM, and a more industrial techno under the name 3abdelkader. Inspired by his architectural, social, and historical surroundings, A.K.M is known for remixing Algerian song classics and incorporating traditional instruments into his productions. Alongside other Algerian artists, he aims to showcase the richness of the national folk repertoire and develop a style unique to his region.
  • A.K.M has also contributed to the Algerian music scene by providing opportunities for young local artists to share their artistic content. In 2015, he launched the "Algerian Techno Movement" project, continuing the "Underground Artist Movement" initiated by Dj Bokko. This project aims to invigorate the Algerian underground music scene by promoting and defending local artists.

Darkmate/Harr
  • Artist Name: Darkmate/Harr
  • Profession: DJ/Producer
  • Region: Sidi Bel Abbès, Algeria
  • Bio:
  • Ahmed Yasser is a DJ and producer from Algeria born in 1997, known by his aliases Dark Mate and Harr (حار). He is a Minimal/Techno producer and has released many tracks on his Bandcamp profile. His style is inspired by ambient and micro sounds, as well as strange and unusual elements. He has performed at Algerian underground events like Between Us Concept, Duck It, El Moutanakil, ASWATT Paris, HIGHDR8, Grooveuak, and Boardeall.
  • Ahmed is the founder of the Boardeall concept and HIGHDR8, an underground music event. He is a member of the Algerian Techno Movement crew and REBELZ. He initially started with his alias Dark Mate, which has a darker universe, such as techno, minimal techno, and dark deep techno. Later, he began playing with his new alias HARR, which has a minimal space.
  • He started producing at a young age with both aliases, minimal and dark techno, and has signed with major international labels like Low Groove Records, Datagroove Records, Habitat Label, and Substrate Music. His work has been supported by great artists and DJs such as Duky, Luixar KL, Darkrow, Butane (Little Helpers), Paco Osuna, Wade, Stefano Noferini, Cosmjn, Arapu, Lizz, Groovebox, and many more.
Faël
  • Artist Name: Faël
  • Profession: DJ
  • Region: Algiers, Algeria
  • Bio:
  • Faël is an electronic music DJ specializing in Hardtechno and Industrial genres, driven by her deep passion for these styles. Her music reflects her fascination with the rapidly evolving aspects of modern society, and her sets are characterized by high-energy and hyperactivity, perfectly embodying her anti-conformist personality.
  • Behind an enigmatic face mask, Faël skillfully manipulates her mixer's knobs to create mesmerizing and thought-provoking sounds. Her sets possess a sense of mystery, and her music often conveys an intriguing message open to interpretation.
  • Faël's love for music began early in life, entranced by her mother's piano playing. Later, in high school, she discovered electronic music and became captivated by the techno scene. It was through DJing and mixing that Faël found the creative freedom she had been seeking.
  • Inspired by artists such as Rebekah, Octave One, and Kashpitzky, Faël has developed a distinctive style that sets her apart from other DJs in her genre. With her unique blend of Hardtechno and Industrial beats, she has quickly gained a following among fans who appreciate her innovative approach to electronic music.
INJECT31 KIMAHAK
  • Artist Name: Kimahak/INJECT31
  • Profession: DJ/Producer
  • Region: Oran, Algeria
  • Bio:
  • Noufel "INJECT31" Bouabdallah, also known as "Kimahak," is an Algerian underground techno music artist based in Spain. As a DJ, producer, and composer, Noufel has made significant strides in the electronic music world. His supportive father played a crucial role in nurturing his passion for music from the age of 8. At 16, Noufel began to explore tribal tech under the alias "NEEYO," performing in his local nightclub.
  • His interest in the underground techno scene grew as he found inspiration from artists such as Luis Flores, Jeff Mills, Oscar Mulero, Reeko, and Speedy J. Noufel's background working in a plastic factory exposed him to industrial sounds, which influenced his sample creation and sound design. This experience led to the development of his first alias, "INJECT31," representing an injection of techno into Oran, his hometown (with the administrative circumscription number 31).
  • Under the INJECT31 alias, Noufel has released music on renowned record labels worldwide, such as Traum TG, Vnderstand Recordings, and Apex Recordings, owned by legendary techno artist Henry Cullen (D.A.V.E The Drummer). His sound is characterized by industrial hard techno with a unique blend of Algerian samples.
  • "Kimahak" is Noufel's most recent alias, showcasing his love for hypnotic and repetitive sounds. Under this name, he has released music on labels like Trau-ma (Robert Limited) and Lkemia (Algerian Techno Movement Sub-Label), where he experiments with techno and Intelligent Dance Music.
Toxics
  • Artist Name: Toxics
  • Profession: DJ/Producer
  • Region: Algiers, Algeria
  • Bio:
  • Datoussaid Abdelilah, known by his stage name 'Toxics,' is an electronic music producer, composer, and sound designer based in Algiers. For Abdelilah, creating music serves as an escape from life's challenges and a means to express deep and vulnerable emotions.
  • Abdelilah discovered DJing at the age of twenty, but soon realized it wasn't his passion. Instead, he returned to composing and producing music across various genres, from Ambient, Background Sound, Techno, Minimal Techno, and Dub Techno to Deep House and Tech House. He has been heavily influenced by a diverse range of artists, including Ricardo Villalobos, Livio & Roby, J.Dilla, Damu The Fudgemunk, and Nujabes. Abdelilah has also experimented with sampling to create 90s Hip Hop vibes.
  • Since 2016, Toxics has been an active member of the "Algerian Techno Movement" collective, where he connected with some of the pioneers of Algerian electronic music, such as A.K.M, INJECT31, and Astro Deepak. These artists guided him on his journey to discover various styles within the electronic music landscape.
  • Today, Toxics is well-known for his Dub Techno, Ambient, and Hip Hop tracks. As he continues to explore his artistic talents, it remains to be seen which genres he will touch upon in the coming years.
Xavo
  • Artist Name: Xavo
  • Profession: DJ/Producer
  • Region: Algiers, Algeria
  • Bio:
  • Xavo, a 25 year old music producer and DJ, spent most of his teenage and young adult years in Algiers. He developed a passion for electronic music at a young age, experimenting with an old family-owned generic CASIO synthesizer. Influenced by various artists, Xavo taught himself DJing, digital audio workstation (DAW) usage, and music production, eventually creating emotive compositions that encompass a blend of different genres, rhythms, and grooves.
  • Today, Xavo is one of the many rising artists in the Algerian underground electronic music scene, collaborating with like-minded local producers and DJs. He is focused on releasing music across a wide spectrum of tempos and aims to start his own live mixes. With a curating mentality that embraces risk-taking, Xavo creates intricate, infectious, and captivating sounds, offering a next-level sound experience in his compositions and mixes.

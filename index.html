ATM Logo

ⵔ - symbolizing "Algerian" in the Berber culture

T - representing "Techno" in the Latin alphabet

م - signifying "Movement" in Arabic script

This intentional fusion of the three letters epitomizes the main languages and cultures of Algeria, which serve as sources of inspiration and artistic influence for the group. The blend showcases the diverse richness of the collective's members, seamlessly merging the East, West, and Africa into a singular, unique identity
